Community Resources

If you are in need of resources (food/water/shelter/rental or utility assistance) please call the below numbers:

The Ministry Center: 501-358-6098
Salvation Army: 501-329-1712
American Red Cross: 501-748-1021
CAPCA: 501-329-0977

If this is an emergency, dial 911. If you are in need of emergency services (flooding/tornado/fire) please call your local Sherrif's Office, County Judge, or the Office of Emergency Management.
